    Frequently Asked Questions

    How is patient data protected?

    All data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption both in transit and at rest. We maintain HIPAA compliance with regular security audits, and all uploaded files are automatically deleted after 24 hours. No patient data is used for model training without explicit consent.

    Can this replace clinical ECG interpretation?

    No. ECG Assistant is designed as a clinical decision support tool to augment, not replace, professional medical judgment. All results should be reviewed by qualified healthcare providers before making clinical decisions.

    What ECG formats are supported?

    We support standard 12-lead ECGs in JPG, PNG, and PDF formats. Images should be clear and complete, showing all leads with minimal artifacts. The system works best with standard paper speeds (25mm/s) and amplitudes (10mm/mV).

    What's the difference between the demo and full version?

    The demo uses sample ECG data to demonstrate our analysis capabilities. The full version allows you to upload actual patient ECGs, provides detailed clinical reports, maintains analysis history, and includes collaboration features.

    How accurate is the AI analysis?

    Our models are validated against clinician consensus with sensitivity and specificity rates exceeding 95% for major abnormalities. Each result includes confidence scores to help interpret the reliability of specific findings.

    What kind of support is available?

    We offer tiered support based on your plan: email support for Starter, priority support with 24-hour response for Professional, and dedicated account management with phone support for Enterprise customers.
